🏛️ System Mutation Alive Interoperability Era

Mega Orchestrator Evolution

The central species in the ecosystem. It started as a useful but brittle coordinator and evolved into a tool router, protocol negotiator, and compatibility layer for multiple AI clients.

Before

Too many assumptions: localhost routing, fake universal endpoints, health checks detached from reality, custom bridge only.

Mutation

Adapters, route awareness, MCP compatibility, resources/templates, and honest tool discovery made it legible to real clients.

Now

It can serve both custom bridge clients and MCP-native clients without lying about what tools it actually exposes.

Why this exhibit matters

This is where engineering pressure did its job. The orchestrator did not improve because of abstract architecture purity. It improved because clients failed, tools misrouted, and the system had to adapt to reality. That is the actual shape of evolution in software: repeated confrontation with the environment.
