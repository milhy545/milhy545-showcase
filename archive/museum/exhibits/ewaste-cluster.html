E-Waste Cluster

The physical exhibit. Recycled hardware is not a side note. It is the proving ground that shaped the entire software stack.

Why this is in the museum

Most engineers optimize for ideal hardware. This cluster forced optimization against the opposite condition: limited RAM, aging CPUs, messy constraints, and parts that should have been written off. That pressure created better operational habits and sharper code discipline.
